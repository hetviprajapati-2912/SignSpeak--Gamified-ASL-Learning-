What is SignSpeak?

SignSpeak is an innovative AI-powered platform designed to make American Sign Language (ASL) learning accessible, engaging, and fun for everyone. Using advanced machine learning and computer vision technology, we provide real-time ASL recognition and interactive learning experiences.

Our mission is to bridge communication gaps and empower the deaf and hearing communities through technology-enhanced ASL education.
